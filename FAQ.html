Frequently Asked Questions

Accounts and Services

What types of accounts do you offer?

Posh FI offers a variety of accounts, including checking, savings, money market, CDs, and retirement accounts (IRAs, 401k).

What are the fees associated with your accounts?

Account fees at Posh FI are minimal, with many accounts offering no monthly maintenance fees. Specific fees can be found in our fee schedule.

Are there any monthly maintenance fees?

Most Posh FI accounts do not have monthly maintenance fees, but some accounts may have fees depending on the balance or type.

What is the minimum balance requirement?

The minimum balance requirement varies by account type. Checking accounts generally have no minimum balance, while savings and money market accounts may have specific requirements.

Do you offer online banking services?

Yes, Posh FI provides comprehensive online banking services, including account management, bill pay, and fund transfers.

Do you have mobile banking apps?

Posh FI offers mobile banking apps for both Android and iOS, allowing you to manage your accounts on the go.

What documents are needed to open an account?

To open an account, you'll need a government-issued ID, proof of address, and your Social Security number.

What is the process to close an account?

To close an account, visit any Posh FI branch or contact our customer service. You may need to settle any outstanding balances.

How do I view my recent transactions?

You can view your recent transactions through our online banking platform or mobile app.

How can I order checks?

Checks can be ordered online through your Posh FI account or by contacting customer service.

How do I update my contact information?

You can update your contact information through online banking, by visiting a branch, or by calling customer service.

How do I sign up for e-statements?

Sign up for e-statements through our online banking portal by navigating to the statements section and opting in.

How can I reset my online banking password?

You can reset your online banking password by clicking "Forgot Password" on the login page and following the instructions.

How can I check my account balance?

Account balances can be checked through online banking, mobile apps, ATMs, or by contacting customer service.
